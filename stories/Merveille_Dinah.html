I wonder if you are doing well, though I have heard the sad news about your wife. The lost of her should've caused so much sadness on you, I cannot even fathom it. I'm really sorry to hear about this. I know you loved her dearly.

I imagine your mind is full of dark thought and you feel hollow. But dear friend of mine. Even though your partner has left you so early in this world, You cannot think about giving up. You still have a daughter to rise, which is not a easy task at all. I don't want to bother with you so many words so I will keep this letter short and straight.

You know that I do also have daughter, which is almost the same age yours. And for the sake of her growing up in a simple life I am living in a small town of Cantellia, albeit my wife isn't happy being around here. My daughter happens to lack playmates in her age. So come here with your little one.

That's was the letter William has recieved while he was drinking himself out in a bar. Since the day her wife's illness reached to a fatal degree and with no possible cure he was already drinking.

"Heh! He does not even ask if I want to go to such a place! Giving me an order so that I put my ass together." he said in a mocking tone, but his lips still curled up a little.

Meanwhile Dinah, (the poor daughter who has lost her mother and with a drunkard father) was drying some cups with a dry rag. She somewhat in this situation become a little worker in the bar and friends with the daughter of the bar (with about 15 year age difference).

"Dinah! Come here, I do have some news for you."

Dinah, who was so focused to dry the cups got irked, almost dropping the cup. But she is a big girl at the age of four, so she wouldn't drop a cup. She put down (or up since she is to short) the cup to the table. With some pitter-patter sound she darted near.

William give the letter to Dinah, but the little girl just looked at her father as asking what is she supposed to do with this paper.

Which made Dinah even more confused. She knew there is a concept of reading but the problem is she still didn't learn such skill. She knows how to count the cups. She also knows how to dry them. But she doesn't know how to read images on paper, yet.

At that point an angry girl, who is still not married even though has reached the age of 19, come to the father-daughter duo.
